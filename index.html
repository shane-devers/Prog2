Sugar-Free Chocolate Cake
A delicious, easy to bake chocolate cake that's ready in less than 5 hours
25th February 2019 baker213
Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 3/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 3/4 cups artificial sweetener
  • 2 eggs
  • 3oz unsweetened chocolate, melted and cooled
  • 1 tsp vanilla
